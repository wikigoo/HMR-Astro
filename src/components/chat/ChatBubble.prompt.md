**HMR ChatBubble** — one message in the advisor chat. `assistant` is glass on the leading edge; `user` is a neon-tinted bubble on the trailing edge.

```jsx
<ChatBubble role="assistant">Based on current Iranian-market data, an iPhone 13 (128GB) runs about 95–110M Tomans.</ChatBubble>
<ChatBubble role="user">Is that fair after 18 months of use?</ChatBubble>
```

Props: `role`, `rtl` (Persian), `style`.
